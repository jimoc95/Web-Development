Game Instructions

  • The aim of the game is to catch as many horses as you can before the enemies catch you.
  • The horses run away from the player when they get a certain distance to the player.
  • The enemies will begin to follow the player around the canvas once they are a certain distance from the player.
  • Every time you collect a ruby the player's speed is increased
  • Every time you catch a horse another enemy is added.
  • Players, enemies and horses are all rebounded from the objects on the map (hole, walls, castle, rocks)
  • Once you have been caught the game will give you your score (The number of horses you have caught before the enemies catch you).
